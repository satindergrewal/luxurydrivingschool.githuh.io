Cancellation Policy

We understand that sometimes schedules can change, and you may need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. To ensure efficient scheduling and accommodate other clients, we kindly request adherence to the following cancellation policy:

  1. First Session Fee: All bookings require prepayment of the first session fee. Please note that your reservation is only confirmed upon receipt of this payment.
  2. Appointment Confirmation: Please confirm your appointment within 24 hours of receiving our appointment reminder. This helps us ensure that your slot is reserved and ready for you.
  3. Cancellation or Rescheduling: If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, we ask that you do so at least 48 hours in advance. This allows us to offer the appointment slot to other clients who may be waiting.
  4. Late Cancellations: Cancellations made within 48 hours of the scheduled appointment time may be subject to a cancellation fee.
  5. No-Shows: If you do not show up for your appointment and have not provided prior notice, you may be charged a no-show fee.
  6. Exceptions: We understand that emergencies and unforeseen circumstances can occur. If you have a valid reason for a late cancellation or no-show, please contact us as soon as possible to discuss your situation.

Our goal is to provide you with the best possible service while respecting the time and schedules of our clients and staff. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation in adhering to this policy.
